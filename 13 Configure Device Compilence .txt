Exercise create device compliance policy

	- Microsoft itune uses device compliance policies to protect your organization's mobile and windows devices.
	- Compliance policies indicate the rules and device settings that should be setup on a device to be considered compliant.
	- After set up and deployment of compliance policy.
	- you can monitor the copliance status and devices that are configured based on the policy settings.
	- before applying a compliance policy to a device, it must first be registered to intune either by joining it to Azure AD or enrollment into mobile device management.
