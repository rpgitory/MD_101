Multi-factor Authentication
	: Implementing MFA in Azure AD is straightforward as it does not require additional services or security infrastructure in the local network.
	: MFA included with Azure premium or enterprise + security mobility or microsoft365 business premium.
	: An admin enters the phone number and email address details in the user's authentication methods property and enables the user for MFA
